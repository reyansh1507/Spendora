What is Spendora?

Spendora is a fully client-side personal finance tracker that runs entirely in your browser — no servers, no cloud sync, no data leaving your device. Everything is stored locally, keeping your financial data 100% private. It gives you a real-time picture of where your money goes, whether it's a morning coffee or a yearly software subscription.

📊
Dashboard
A live overview of your monthly budget, total spending, and daily activity — including a calendar heatmap and smart stat cards that update the moment you add an expense.
💸
Expense Tracking
Log daily expenses by category (Food, Transport, Health, Entertainment, and more) with inline editing. Navigate between any past date and instantly see what you spent and on what.
🔁
Subscription Manager
Track recurring charges — streaming, music, cloud, gaming, fitness — across monthly, yearly, quarterly, or weekly billing cycles. Know exactly what renews and when, so nothing catches you off guard.
📈
Analytics
Bar charts for the last 6 months of spending, donut breakdowns by category, subscription cost trends, and budget utilisation bars — all rendered in-browser with animated SVG.
🔔
Smart Alerts
An intelligent alert engine flags budget overruns, upcoming subscription renewals (within 5 days), high-subscription load, and daily spending spikes — colour-coded by urgency from critical to all-clear.
🎯
Budget Goals
Set a monthly spending limit and watch Spendora track every rupee against it in real time — with progress bars, percentage used, and a clear remaining balance at a glance.
⚙️

How It Works

01
Sign Up or Demo
Create a free account with your email and a password, or jump straight in with the demo credentials to explore the full interface.
02
Set a Budget
Click "Set Budget" in the navbar and enter your monthly spending limit in ₹. Spendora will track everything against it automatically.
03
Log Expenses Daily
Head to the Expenses page, pick a date, and add rows for what you spent. Categories and amounts update your dashboard in real time.
04
Add Subscriptions
Enter your recurring services in the Subscriptions page. Spendora tracks renewal dates and includes them in your monthly cost calculations.
05
Review Analytics
Use the Analytics page to spot trends, understand category breakdowns, and compare months — so you can make smarter financial decisions going forward.
🔒
Your Data Stays With You
Spendora stores everything in your browser's localStorage. No data is ever sent to a server, no accounts are stored remotely, and no third-party analytics track your usage. Clear your browser storage and everything is gone — you're always in full control.
